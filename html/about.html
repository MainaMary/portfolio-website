    Product designer

    I am Ola, a Product Designer living in Canada. With a solid 3+ years in the field, I am passionate about crafting designs that bring ideas to life in a user-friendly way. Beyond my design journey, you'll often find me exploring the realms of art and technology.

    I actually started off my career as a digital marketer, eventually discovered Product design. I slowly drifted into the design world and I never looked back.

    I thrive on bridging creativity and functionality to create memorable user experiences. When I'm not immersed in design, you can catch me savoring delicious food or seeking inspiration in the world around me. Let's connect and embark on a design adventure together!
